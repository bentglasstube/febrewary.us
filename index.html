FeBREWary 2014
Saturday, March 1, 2pm - 6pm

Join us for the first annual FeBREWary! Several people will be brewing homemade beer and everyone that attends gets to judge which one is best. After the judging, try to drink all of your favorite one before anyone else gets to enjoy it or just chat with the other attendees or whatever you normally do at parties.

You don't even have to bring anything, just show up and help determine who has the best shot at quitting his or her day job and starting a brewery.
